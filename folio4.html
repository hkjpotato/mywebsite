Medicine Reminder



About

Medicine Reminder is a platform to help a medicine taker and his/her care giver to better manager the medicine taking acitivities. The platform mainly involves an IOS application for a care giver to track the medicine taking condition remotely, and a touch screen interface on the medicine container to remind the medicine taker about their pills.


Implementation

Direct interviews as well as personas development were conducted to have an insight of the user scenarios. Nuanced thinking on interaction flows and interface design ensured the ease of use of the product.


IOS APP

Low fidelity prototype was first developed by Balsamiq. Then, Keynote is used to developed the interactive prototype with animation effect and details such as color. (Why Keynote?)

Keynote is adopted by company like Apple UX team to develope fast and interactive prototypes (example). I am a Keynote lover. For me, the most important feature of keynote is the "magic move" function, which requires connections between elements of two successive interfaces. This helps designer to consciously formulate a clear interaction flow. (I even used keynote to explain an ambiguous string searching algorithm for my algorithm class).


Touchscreen Interface

As a course project, we were asked to develope in a different style other than the IOS app. Thus I adopted a Material Design style for the touch screen interface. I used Keynote for the UI design, and Axure for the interactive prototype.
